AROMA Coffee & Tea is proud to be locally owned and operated serving award-winning, fresh, quality-focused specialty coffee and tea. We offer indoor & outdoor seating, as well as a drive thru window for your convenience. We also provide free Wifi inside. With over ten years of experience in the industry, we source, roast, blend and serve the best coffee in Ohio. We value the importance of fairness and quality at each step of the process from seed to cup.
