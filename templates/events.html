Special Events: Tastings and More

Like wine, there are so many varieties of coffee that tasting them all can be somewhat overwhelming. Guided Tastings allow you to experience the flavor characteristics of a particular coffee and compare it to another coffee from the same or different growing region or processing method.

Every Saturday we invite you to AROMA to experience a guided tasting of our special featured coffees and teas. And during each tasting event, you will receive 15% off on all coffees, teas and equipment featured.
